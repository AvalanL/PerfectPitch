Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my premium subscription?

Go to Profile > Settings > Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription will be restored automatically if you've previously purchased it with the same Apple ID.

Can I use Perfect Pitch offline?

Yes! Perfect Pitch works fully offline. Your progress syncs automatically when you're back online.

How do I change the instrument sound?

Go to Settings > Sound > Instrument to choose from piano, guitar, violin, synth, and more. Premium users have access to additional instruments.

What is a streak freeze?

A streak freeze protects your practice streak if you miss a day. You can earn streak freezes through achievements or purchase them in the app.

How do daily challenges work?

Daily challenges are curated 5-minute exercises that adapt to your skill level. Complete them to earn bonus XP and maintain your streak.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Go to your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage or cancel your Perfect Pitch subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
