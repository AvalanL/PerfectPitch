Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Introduction

Perfect Pitch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: Perfect Pitch does not access your microphone, does not record audio, and prioritizes your privacy at every step.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address (if using email sign-up)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)

If you sign in with Apple, we only receive the information you choose to share.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about how you use the app:

  • Training progress and scores
  • Achievements earned
  • Practice streaks and statistics
  • App feature usage patterns
  • Device type and operating system version

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Audio recordings - we never access your microphone
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or calendar information
  • Photos or media (unless you upload a profile picture)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Perfect Pitch service
  • Track your learning progress and statistics
  • Personalize your training experience
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Display leaderboards (if you opt in)
  • Send practice reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Respond to support requests

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security audits are performed

Your practice data can be used offline and syncs securely when you're connected to the internet.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics services that help us improve the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Leaderboards: Your display name and scores may be visible to other users if you opt into leaderboards

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics or leaderboard participation

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@perfectpitch.app

Children's Privacy

Perfect Pitch is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase/Supabase: For authentication and data storage
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve features
  • Apple App Store: For purchases and subscriptions

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing their use of data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@perfectpitch.app

Support: perfectpitch.app/support
